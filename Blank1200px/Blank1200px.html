Kirk Whitsitt

Kirk Whitsitt is a student at Illinois State University, majoring in audio and music production, with a music minor. They aim to work in production for live music, studio recording, and continue making their own music as well. Kirk currently works at WGLT, a local non-profit public radio station, as the editor of WGLT's Sound Ideas, starting in August of 2025.
